# 📊 Customer Churn Insights

## Key Drivers of Churn
- Customers with short tenure are more likely to churn.
- Higher number of complaints strongly predicts churn.
- Lower revenue customers tend to leave more often.

## Observations
- Regions show different churn patterns.
- Customer dissatisfaction (complaints) is the strongest signal.

## Recommendations
- 🎯 Target customers with low tenure using onboarding support.
- 📞 Improve customer service to reduce complaints.
- 💰 Offer discounts or loyalty programs for low-revenue customers.

## Business Actions
- Implement early warning system for at-risk customers.
- Personalize retention campaigns based on behavior.
